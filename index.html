The problem

You're building products for people who don't exist.

§ 01 Why traditional research fails founders

Surveys tell you what people think they want. Focus groups produce consensus, not truth. Research agencies deliver 6-week reports about a market that already moved.

Meanwhile, millions of people are publicly, vocally, emotionally describing exactly what they need — in Reddit threads, Twitter rants, Hacker News comment sections, G2 reviews. The signal is everywhere. The problem is extraction.

  • 01 Surveys measure stated intent, not revealed behavior. 70% of people say they'd pay for something, 3% actually do.
  • 02 Research agencies cost $10k+ and take 6 weeks. By the time you get results, the window has closed.
  • 03 Manually reading forums doesn't scale. You miss patterns. You can't rank intensity. You can't track velocity.
